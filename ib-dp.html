The programme

Rigour with a point.

Six subjects across the disciplines, plus the three core elements — the Extended Essay, Theory of Knowledge, and Creativity, Activity, Service. Students don't just take exams; they research, argue, and act. It is the closest thing secondary school offers to the demands of university.

i.
The Extended Essay

An independent, 4,000-word piece of research — a student's first real thesis, planned, argued, and defended.

ii.
Theory of Knowledge

A course in how we know what we know — interrogating evidence, perspective, and the limits of each discipline.

iii.
Creativity, Activity, Service

Learning carried beyond the classroom — into creative work, physical challenge, and service to the community.
