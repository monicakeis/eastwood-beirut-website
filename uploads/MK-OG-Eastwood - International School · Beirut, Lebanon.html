◼ A typical day

This is what your child’s Tuesday looks like.

Because brochures lie. Here’s the actual schedule — the one that shapes the habits that shape the life.

Primary (PYP · ages 6–11)
  1. 07:45
    Mindful morning
    Five-minute guided meditation. Sets the tone before academics begin.
  2. 08:00
    Inquiry block
    Student-led inquiry into this unit’s transdisciplinary theme (e.g. "How the world works").
  3. 10:15
    Language
    Arabic, French or English — depending on your child’s pathway. All three offered.
  4. 11:30
    STEM lab
    Hands-on science with our Apple-partnered iPad lab. No worksheets.
  5. 12:30
    Fresh-meal lunch
    On-site kitchen. Nutritionist-designed, Mediterranean-forward menu.
  6. 13:30
    Specialist
    Music, art, PE, or library. Rotates daily.
  7. 14:45
    Reflection
    Portfolio time. Students document what they learned — not what they did.
  8. 15:15
    After-school
    40+ clubs: chess, coding, football, Model UN, robotics, Duke of Edinburgh.

◼ How enrollment works

21 days from curious
to confirmed.

Most schools take two months to reply. We made admissions feel like the rest of modern life — fast, clear, and human.

  1. 01
    Minute one
    Tell us about your child
    Start with the 90-second fit quiz. You’ll receive a personalized fit report by email — no form, no call required to read it.
  2. 02
    Within 7 days
    Campus visit or Zoom
    A 45-minute conversation with the Head of Admissions. You’ll meet the Principal and see classrooms in action.
  3. 03
    Within 14 days
    Student assessment
    Age-appropriate. Playful for Early Years, academic for Diploma. Never adversarial. Your child will enjoy it — that’s the point.
  4. 04
    Within 21 days
    Decision & enrollment
    Offer letter, welcome package, and your family liaison’s contact. Visa letters, uniform fitting, bus routes — we handle them.
Our admissions promise.We respond to every inquiry within one business day. If we can’t, your application fee is waived. That’s the Eastwood guarantee.
